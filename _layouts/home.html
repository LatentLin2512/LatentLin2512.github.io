🖉 About Me

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Engineering at ZJU-UIUC Institute. I am currently exploring my academic interests. At this stage, I am particularly curious about embodied intelligence, with a focus on Vision–Language Models (VLMs) and Vision–Language–Action (VLA) models.

Outside of research, I am passionate about science communication and content creation, especially in making complex scientific ideas accessible to a broader audience. I was also an Olympiad in Informatics (OI) participant for many years during high school, which taught me a lot about algorithms, persistence, and losing gracefully. 😅
